Introduction

The series of interactive network graphs linked below are intended to help visualize the connections among people and works in Cather's correspondence. While neither definitive nor exhaustive, these graphs are intended to provide dynamic ways of contextualizing Cather within her social and artistic networks. Each link will take you to an interactive graph as well as a brief description of the graph and a summary of how it was made. The data underlying these graphs was made possible by the Willa Cather Archive's endeavors to digitize Cather's complete correspondence. The ability to visualize Cather's references to people and places is possible because of the Cather Archive's decision to richly encode each letter in XML.
